Regression with dropout uncertainty demo

Simple regression problem. Current stochastic forward pass through the dropout network is plotted in black and the mean of the last 100 forward passes is plotted in blue. Shades of blue denote half a standard deviation. You can click on the plot to add new data points and see how the uncertainty changes! Try adding a group of new points to the far right and see how the uncertainty changes between these and the points at the centre (you might have to restart the network after adding new points).
